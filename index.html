About Our Company

Metascrap is a leading scrap collection service provider in Mexico, specializing in the efficient and responsible collection of various types of scrap materials. With a commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility, we offer our services to maquiladoras across Juarez, Chihuahua, and the surrounding areas. Our team of professionals is dedicated to providing top-notch service, ensuring that our clients receive the best value for their scrap materials while contributing to a greener future.

Our services include the collection of iron, steel, aluminum, copper, bronze, lead, zinc, and electronic scrap. We operate from Monday to Friday, from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, providing flexible scheduling options to meet the needs of our clients. We also offer convenient payment methods, including bank transfers and cash payments upon delivery. At Metascrap, we are committed to delivering efficient, secure, and responsible service that supports recycling efforts and promotes environmental sustainability. Contact us today for more information or to request a quote.
