About MetaScrapMX

Company Description


MetaScrapMX provides scrap collection services for maquiladoras, industrial plants, and businesses that need a reliable partner to keep their operations clean, organized, and compliant with responsible recycling practices. Our work is focused on materials recovery, operational support, and simple communication with every client.


We handle the collection of ferrous and non-ferrous materials, as well as electronic scrap, with an approach that prioritizes punctuality, clear coordination, and safe handling. The goal is to make the process easy for the client while creating value from the materials that are no longer needed on site.

Our History


MetaScrapMX was created with a simple idea: industrial scrap collection should be dependable, transparent, and adapted to the rhythm of the client’s operation. From the beginning, the company was shaped around direct service, practical solutions, and long-term working relationships with maquiladoras in Juarez and the surrounding area.


Over time, that focus has helped us build a service model centered on consistency and trust. We continue improving our processes so we can respond faster, coordinate better pickups, and support clients who need a partner that understands industrial timelines and production demands.

Operating Hours


Our service hours are Monday to Friday, from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM. This schedule allows us to coordinate pickups during active business hours and keep communication aligned with plant supervisors, logistics teams, and purchasing departments.


If your operation requires scheduled collection or specific timing for material release, we work to coordinate those details in advance. The objective is to keep your workflow moving without interruptions while making scrap removal as simple as possible.

How We Support Clients


We support clients by offering clear communication, fast response times, and a straightforward process from the first contact to the final pickup. Whether you need a one-time collection or ongoing support, we keep the process practical and easy to manage.


Our support also includes help with service requests, scheduling questions, and basic guidance about the type of material being collected. If you need a quote or want to coordinate a pickup, you can contact us directly and we will help you move forward with the next step.

With a commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility, MetaScrapMX offers efficient and responsible scrap collection services to maquiladoras across Juarez, Chihuahua, and the surrounding areas.
